Online Admission

A complete digital solution for managing school admissions, designed to make the enrollment process smooth and paperless for parents.

Online Form: Comprehensive digital application collecting student history and parent details.
Eligibility Table: Instant age and grade requirement verification for applicants.
Document Checklist: Smart upload portal for Birth Certificates, Transcripts, and ID photos.
Prospectus Download: High-speed download link for the latest school brochure and rules.

How it Works

**Smart Validation:** The system ensures all mandatory fields and documents are provided before submission, reducing administrative follow-up work by 70%.
